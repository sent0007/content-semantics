55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest the root

The usual deep purple roots of beetrootare eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.
